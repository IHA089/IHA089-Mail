IHA089-Mail

Experience a realistic environment designed to enhance your ethical hacking and penetration testing skills. As part of our lab, we’ve integrated a custom email service, allowing you to create and log in with your unique email address (e.g., example@iha089.org).

This email system is hosted locally and tailored specifically for IHA089 Vulnerable Labs, offering a secure yet realistic platform for testing and experimentation. The email address you create here will be used within the labs to perform tasks such as account creation, login, signup confirmation, and other challenges designed to simulate real-world scenarios.

Receive mails for login or signup confirmation, or for other tasks as you navigate through and solve the challenges in IHA089 Vulnerable Labs.
